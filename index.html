Why Amsterdam?

Amsterdam, the city of history

The Amsterdam Van Gogh Museum has on display around 200 paintings and drawings and also, some 750 letters of the Dutch painter Vincent van Gogh. The Amsterdam Van Gogh Museum holds the largest collection of works by this 19th-century painter.

The Van Gogh Museum in Amsterdam houses the largest collection of artworks by Vincent van Gogh (1853-1890) in the world. The permanent collection includes over 200 paintings by Vincent van Gogh, 500 drawings and more than 750 letters.
